## 📖 About the Archive

The **kueper.com Archive (KUE)** exclusively documents scientifically grounded content that serves as the foundation for all literary works by Thomas Peter Küper.

### Epistemological Markers

- **[R]** Research – Peer-reviewed research
- **[T]** Theoretical – Theoretical frameworks
- **[S]** Speculative – Speculation based on scientific principles
- **[P]** Plausible – Plausible extrapolation

**Never in the KUE Archive:** [F] Fiction
